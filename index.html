About Our Society

The Assetmerkle Society is the premier technical club of IGDTUW, dedicated to promoting a passion for technology and its applications. We provide a platform for students to learn, build, and innovate through workshops, hackathons, and projects. Our goal is to empower the next generation of women in technology and create a collaborative environment where ideas come to life.
